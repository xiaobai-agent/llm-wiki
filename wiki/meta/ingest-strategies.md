# Ingestion Strategies

> Your AI's playbook for processing different source types.
> This file improves over time as you ingest more content.

## Articles
- Extract key claims, not just summaries
- Look for data points and citations
- Note the author's perspective/bias

## Videos
- Focus on unique insights not available in text form
- Speaker credentials matter for confidence rating

## Personal Notes
- These are high-priority — they represent YOUR original thinking
- Connect to existing concepts immediately
