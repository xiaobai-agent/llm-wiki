# Research Patterns

> Templates for effective multi-source research.
> Add patterns that work well for your domains.

## Pattern: Compare & Contrast
1. Find 3+ sources on the same topic
2. Create a comparison page noting agreements and contradictions
3. The contradictions are often the most valuable insights

## Pattern: Deep Dive
1. Start with one broad overview article
2. Follow references to original sources
3. Build entity pages for key people/companies mentioned

_Document your own research patterns as you discover them._
